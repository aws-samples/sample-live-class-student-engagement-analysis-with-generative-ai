# E2E Testing of live demo

After deploying the IVS UGC demo, a CloudFormation stack will be created. Follow these steps to access the application:

Step 1. Go to the CloudFormation console and locate the stack named **UGC dev**.

![Fig 7](../asset/demo/image1.png)

Step 2. Click on the Outputs tab, scroll down, and find the frontendAppBaseUrl. Use this URL to open the login page.

![Fig 8](../asset/demo/image2.png)

Step 3. Create a user account and login to access the home screen.

![Fig 9](../asset/demo/image3.png)

![Fig 10](../asset/demo/image4.png)

It will take you to the home page.

![Fig 11](../asset/demo/image5.png)

Step 4. Navigate to Stream Manager: On the left sidebar, find and click on the second-to-last option, **Stream Manager**.

![Fig 12](../asset/demo/image6.png)

Step 5. Click on **Go Live**. This will take you to the main page where you can share your screen and start streaming. Click on **Start Streaming** after sharing the screen.

![Fig 13](../asset/demo/image7.png)

![Fig 14](../asset/demo/image8.png)

![Fig 15](../asset/demo/image9.png)


Step 7. Click the top icon **View Your Channel**. This will display the student screen, allowing you to see your slides along with questions generated from screenshots captured every few seconds.

Teacher View:

![Fig 16](../asset/demo/image10.png)

Student View:

![Fig 17](../asset/demo/image11.png)

As you can see here, the question is being generated from the same slide that was shared by the teacher.
